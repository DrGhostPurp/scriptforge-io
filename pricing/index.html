Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free tier really free forever?
Yes. The Free tier includes the full IDE, all 8 tools, and unlimited local scripts with no time limit or credit card required. It will remain free permanently. We make money on Pro and Team plans, not by paywalling the core IDE.
When will Pro and Team launch?
We're building Pro now. Join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know. Everyone on the waitlist gets a 30-day free Pro trial when we launch.
Does my code get sent to a server?
On the Free tier, nothing ever leaves your browser. Python runs via Pyodide (WebAssembly), JavaScript runs natively, and scripts are saved to localStorage. On Pro/Team, scripts sync to the cloud only when you choose to save them — local execution always stays local.
What Python packages are available?
Pyodide ships with 100+ pre-compiled packages including NumPy, pandas, matplotlib, scikit-learn, SciPy, Pillow, regex, and many more. The full list is available on the Pyodide documentation site.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Both Pro and Team are monthly subscriptions with no contracts. Cancel any time and you retain access until the end of your billing period.
